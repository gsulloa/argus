Argus inspects and edits data across Postgres, DynamoDB, and CloudWatch from a single desktop. The design system below makes the mythology operational: dark-first, hairline-precise, opinionated.

Color · Argus violet on a near-black canvas

Single accent. Restrained semantics. The accent is pulled from the logo gradient (#A855F7) so the brand earns its place in the product, not just on the marketing page. Light mode shifts to #7C3AED for AA contrast on warm-white surfaces.

Typography · Geist + Geist Mono

One family, two faces. Geist for everything UI; Geist Mono everywhere data lives. Tabular numerals are on by default so columns of numbers line up without effort. No Inter, no SF Pro, no Söhne — Geist is free, crisp at 13px on macOS, and signals taste without screaming.
